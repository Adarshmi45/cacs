About Us

The Creative Arts and Cultural Society (CACS) is a student-run organization at the Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) Kota. It was founded in 2013 with the goal of providing a platform for students to explore their creative and cultural interests. The society oversees a range of activities, including performing arts, visual arts, literary arts, and technical events. In addition to these general activities, CACS also has several clubs that cater to specific interests, such as dance, music, theater, art, photography, literature, coding, and robotics. CACS is a great way for students to get involved in the cultural and technical life of IIIT Kota. The society offers a variety of activities to choose from, and there is something for everyone. If you are interested in getting involved in CACS, you can visit the society's website or social media pages for more information.
